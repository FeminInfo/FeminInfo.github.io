Periods

Fast Facts: -Menstruation is the monthly shedding of the lining of the uterus -Menstruation usually lasts 3-5 days (don’t worry: anywhere from 2-7 days is completely normal) -The average age to get your period in the US is 8 to 12 years old -Tampons and pads can be used to control bleeding Health Risks: -TSS (Toxic Shock Syndrome) is a bacterial disease often associated with tampon use. -It is advisable to seek medical attention if: ~ Your period lasts for more than a week ~ Your blood soaks through more than one pad/tampon every 1-2 hours ~ More than 3 months pass between periods ~ You bleed in between periods ~ You have unusual pain before/during/after your period ~ Your regular periods become irregular Sources and Resources- Web MD Medicine Net Kids Health Kids Health
